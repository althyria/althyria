#### Research Direction

Developing reflective technology and trauma-informed systems for survivors and children.

**Focus areas:**
* Constitutional AI supporting safe symbolic thinking and complete user autonomy
* Reflective journaling infrastructure for e-ink devices  
* Structured language frameworks preventing reality distortion and diagnostic confusion

**Languages:** C, Rust, Nix, Haskell

**Technical foundation:** Systems programming, declarative infrastructure, functional programming.
